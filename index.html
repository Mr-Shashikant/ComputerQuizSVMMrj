Rules [Session 2024-25]

  • There will be a group of 03 students in each varg.
  • There will be 10 rounds in quiz.
  • Highest marks scorer teams will be declared for 1st, 2nd and 3rd position accordingly.
  • In case of tie, extra round of 03 questions will be provided among the same higher scorer teams.
  • In case of still tie, extra round of one question will be provided among the higher scorer teams till the result.
  • In case of presenting questions using projector screen, questions will not be spoken. Time counter will start while displaying the question on the screen.
  • Questions will be in English language only.
  • Answer the question within 30 seconds.
  • Late answer will not be accepted.
  • Change in first answer is not allowed.
  • In case of any circumstance, subject convenerâ€™s decision will be acceptable.
  • Lok Shiksha Samiti, Bihar will have the right to change any rule of computer quiz.
  • The competition will be held only up to the Province level (Prant stara).
